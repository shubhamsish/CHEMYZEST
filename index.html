ABOUT



Welcome to the CHEMYZEST 3.O website! We are thrilled to have you here and excited to share with you all the amazing things that our event has to offer. Our Tech Fest is a celebration of innovation, creativity, and technological advancements with fun. We have designed this event to bring together chemical enthusiasts from whole college to engage in thought-provoking discussions, explore cutting-edge seminars, and participate on exciting events. At our Tech Fest, you will have the opportunity to attend keynote speeches and panel discussions by students of oyr college, participate in hands-on Quizes, and compete in exciting competitions. Our theme this year is focused on the intersection of technology and social good. Through our activities and events, we hope to inspire you to think critically about how you can use technology to make a difference in the world. All the participants will be sharing their insights, experiences, and ideas with you, and we are confident that you will enjoy being participated. We would like to thank HOD sir,and all the faculty memberss , and coordinators for their invaluable contributions towards making this event a success. We are grateful for their support and appreciate their commitment to promoting this event. We hope that you will join us at our Tech Fest and be a part of this incredible celebration of technology and social good. Thank you for visiting our website, and we look forward to seeing you soon!
